The Perfect Apple Pie

A classic homemade apple pie to satisfy your cravings!

Ingredients

  • 6-8 apples, peeled and sliced
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • 1 package pie crusts (or homemade)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, salt, and lemon juice.
  3. Roll out one pie crust in a pie dish and fill with apple mixture.
  4. Dot with butter and cover with the second pie crust, sealing the edges.
  5. Brush with egg wash and cut small slits on top.
  6. Bake for 50-60 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
